About

IBA Tech Academy is an educational initiative of International Bank of Azerbaijan based on the methodology developed by center for training of IT professionals «DAN IT education». The education program is based on the Israeli method for training IT-personnel - Telem.

Currently the Academy provides immersive bootcamp programs covering main areas of coding. Applicants thriving to develop necessary competencies for programming to build their future career in IT industry can be enrolled to the program through successfully completing 4 stages of selection process.

IBA Tech Academy is established within the framework of the “International Bank of Azerbaijan”’s corporate social responsibility strategy.

There is no tuition fee for participation in the program.

IBA Tech Academy’s mission is to develop the Azerbaijani IT market in accordance with technology of the future to achieve technological breakthroughs through cultivating new generation of professionals.

DAN.IT education

DAN.IT education training center was established in 2017 following Israeli model of IT-specialists training. The training course is designed by means of Telem method which favours practical phased approach, mentoring and intensive studies.

DAN.IT students choose between Full Stack, FrontEnd, Interaction (UI/UX design), Digital Marketing and programming courses for teenagers and children are also available. In August 2019 a new course of Software Engineering was launched. Courses are supplemented by weekly seminars and workshops from Ukrainian and International guest-speakers involved in IT-industry who share their practical experiences with students.

The center is supported by the Advisory Board, consisting of Ukrainian and International IT-companies representatives. The Board, along with real customers, propose our students ideas for implementation, from perspective of which students develop and vet projects, tuning soft skills and learning to showcase the results.

The initiators of launching DAN.IT education сenter in Ukraine are known for establishing John Bryce Training school network in Israel, which delivers more then 70% high-tech experts to the country labor market. The training center in Kiev may also boast with great results over 2 years of work: more than 1000 graduates and the employment level has reached to 73%. DAN.IT education plans to start new programs, open branches in the Ukrainian regions and starting a project in Minsk, Belarus.

Training program

Method

The training program is based on the Israeli method for training IT-personnel - Telem. This is a comprehensive individual approach to a student:

In the beginning a candidate is profiled, his strengths and weaknesses are determined , personal motivation is taken into account

A candidate is getting tested and interviewed orally

Step-by-step training: at the start basic skills are studied, then - technology concepts and specific technologies in practice

The key principle is a project-based training, practical exercises evading boring lectures

No lectures and workbooks during classes - only tasks and challenges of various complexity level to be solved

The students will always have a support - the Academy uses a mentoring system

Throughout their studies, students implement projects to consolidate their learning

Training process

Students will be divided into 4 groups, which over the course of 2.5 months will intensively study in 2 different programming areas

Each group will be assigned to the experienced teacher from the same programming area. All the teachers are real experts who work for DAN.IT education and have at least 10 years of experience in the IT industry

The course mentors are also responsible for the students. The mentors task is to address students’ issues regarding technical part of training. In their spare time the mentor and the teacher have to respond students’ requests and help to absorb the material effectively

Groups are supervised by the facilitators. These people are responsible for organizational aspects of training, agenda, course schedule, facilities, equipment, etc. Students may contact their facilitators with a request to solve every day and motivational issues

At the end the best students, who have proven themselves, will receive an employment. The International Bank of Azerbaijan representatives will make job offers

To become a student

1

Registration

On the stage of application submitting all candidates have to fill in personal information and answer a few questions.

2

Adam Milo testing

After registration all applicants receive a test assignment for logical and spatial thinking according to Adam Milo method. Testing on the first stage is designed to evaluate candidate’s ability to perform the objective, think clearly, get creative and attentive to details.

3

Technical testing

Candidates admitted to the second stage are tested for technical abilities. The test shows candidate’s ability to cope with a simple objective and evaluates his or her correspondence to a particular curriculum.

4

Interview and Enrollment

The final stage of selection is a profession-oriented online interview with the course authors. Potential students need to make a introduction, and the committee determine a personal predisposition to learning.

FAQ

What is the best age for training?
You can apply for the program at any age. The key factor for us is your desire to study. However, priority will be given to senior high school students and graduates.
What knowledge background should a candidate have?
You can take the course even being a newbie in programming. Also any additional knowledge add a benefit, both during enrollment and training.
What's the language of the training?
English or russian
Is online course available?
Unfortunately, our bootcamp-programs are designed for your actual presence during classes. Online is not provided. Only offline can guarantee an effective absorbing of knowledge. You have a direct access to your teacher in the class. If you have a question, you receive an immediate answer. That helps a lot taking into account the intensiveness of the course.
What is tuition fee?
Tuition is free of charge. In the case student comes from other country than Azerbaijan to take the course, expenses for accommodation and meals in Baku during training are covered by the student.
What is duration of the training?
Training lasts for 2.5 months.
Whom can I ask for help?
In the process of selection you may address your questions to the program initiators using contacts below. During the course each group is assigned to the mentor and course facilitator, who can help with technical and personal issues.
May bank employees apply for the program?
The project aims to attract new personnel in the field of business and public administration in the field of information technology. The project has social goals rather than being internal bank training program.Therefore, we do not consider the participation of bank employees in the program. The emphasis of our program is the support and development of personnel who are just starting their careers.
